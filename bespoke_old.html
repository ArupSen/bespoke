Bespoke

Every client's requirements are unique. Some have a very precise idea of the piece they want to have made, Others need to start at the beginning and first consider how and when it is to be worn. Often this will dictate which stones we use.

We then discuss a budget and I will choose a selection of stones for you to peruse. Depending on how rare the type of stone, it may be one of a kind, or you may have several stones to select from. Remember each gem has its own characteristics and there will be one which seems to be just made for you. We then spend as long as you need designing how the stones will be set to reflet your personal style and budget.

I had the pleasure of helping two lovely friends ( and the creators of my fab website) to make their dream come true with the perfect engagement ring which is totally unique, modern, quirky, timeless and incredibly special. Alyson and Simon had a very strong design aesthetic and I encouraged them to primarily focus on choosing the perfect stone. We considered yellow and blue Diamonds, all colours of Sapphires and Spinels and finally they settled on the most amazing 11.26ct brilliant cut natural french lemon Sapphire (with a Gem-A lab report) set in a really simple 18ct yellow gold setting to enhance and show off this beautiful stone.
